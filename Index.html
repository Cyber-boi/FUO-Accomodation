📋 Case Study — The Problem

Why students at FUO struggle with off-campus housing

Otuoke is a small Ijaw community. While the campus spans 200 hectares, on-campus hostel capacity cannot absorb all 8,000+ students, pushing many into the surrounding community — where challenges abound.

🚫

No central listing system

Students rely on word-of-mouth, flyers, and social media to find rooms — a slow, unreliable process prone to fraud.

💸

Exploitation by agents

Middlemen inflate prices and charge multiple "agency fees", making already tight student budgets even harder to manage.

⚠️

Safety concerns

Unverified landlords and uninspected properties put students at risk, with no formal complaint or resolution channel.

📏

Distance from campus

Poor transport infrastructure means students unknowingly rent far from campus, losing hours to commuting daily.

How FUO HomeFind works

Designed specifically for the FUO community. Simple, transparent, and free for students.

1

Search & filter

Browse verified listings in Otuoke and nearby areas, filtered by your budget and proximity to FUO.

Payments are held in escrow until you confirm the property matches what was advertised. No fraud.
